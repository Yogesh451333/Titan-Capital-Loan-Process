        <h2>Step 1: Getting Started</h2>
        <h3>1. Receive Your Invitation</h3>
        <p>Check your email for the invitation link sent by Titan Capital. Click on the link to start the registration process.</p>

        <h3>2. Register and Set Up Your Login</h3>
        <p>Enter your details (name, email, etc.) and choose a secure password to complete the registration.</p>

        <h3>3. Log In for the First Time</h3>
        <p>Navigate to the Titan Capital login page and enter your username and password to access the broker dashboard.</p>
        
        <h2>Step 2: Starting a New Loan Application</h2>
        <h3>1. Initiate a New Application</h3>
        <p>Click on “New Application” from the dashboard and choose the type of loan application (e.g., Term Loan, Business Line of Credit, Credit Cards). Select “Term Loan” to proceed.</p>

        <h3>2. Agree to Terms and Conditions</h3>
        <p>Read the terms and conditions and click “Agree” to continue with the application process.</p>

        <h3>3. Enter Property Information</h3>
        <p>Provide details about the property being used as security for the loan. Enter the property’s address and adjust the Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR) if needed.</p>

        <h2>Step 3: Moving Forward with Your Application</h2>
        <h3>1. Review Property Details and Security Type</h3>
        <p>Confirm the property details and select the appropriate security type. If necessary, choose “Other” for a review by the Credit Team.</p>

        <h3>2. Provide Borrower Information</h3>
        <p>Enter details about the borrower, including income sources and existing debts. Add additional property owners if applicable.</p>

        <h2>Step 4: Completing the Loan Application</h2>
        <h3>1. Upload Required Documents</h3>
        <p>Upload documents such as the contract of sale, proof of existence, discharge forms, and IDs directly through the software.</p>

        <h3>2. Final Review and Submission</h3>
        <p>Review all information for accuracy and click “Submit” to finalize and send your application to Titan Capital.</p>

        <h3>3. Immediate Response</h3>
        <p>After submission, the system will provide an immediate response:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Letter of Offer (LOO) Issued: If approved, you’ll receive an LOO.</li>
            <li>Rejected: If not approved, you’ll be notified immediately.</li>
            <li>Pending Credit Review: Your application will be sent to the Credit Team if further review is needed.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Final Steps</h2>
        <p>If approved, the final loan amount will be calculated and relevant parties will be informed. Titan Capital aims to provide a smooth and efficient process for managing loan applications.</p>
