        <h3>Receive Your Invitation</h3>

        <h3>Register and Set Up Your Login</h3>
        <p>Create your account by entering your details and choosing a secure password. Confirm your details to complete registration        <p>Access the Titan Capital platform by logging in with your new credentials on the login page.</p>

        <h2>Step 2: Starting a New Loan Application</h2>
        <h3>Initiate a New Application</h3>
        <p>Start a new loan application from the dashboard by selecting “New Application” and choosing the type of loan (e.g., Term Loan).</p>

        <h3>Agree to Terms and Conditions</h3>
        <p>Review and agree to Titan Capital’s terms and conditions to proceed with the application.</p>

        <h3>Enter Property Information</h3>
        <p>Provide details about the property being used as security. Enter the property address to search our database or manually input details if necessary. Adjust the Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR) or add another property if required.</p>

        <h2>Step 3: Moving Forward with Your Application</h2>
        <h3>Review Property Details and Security Type</h3>
        <p>Verify the property details and select the type of security. If “Other” is selected, the application will be reviewed by the Credit Team.</p>

        <h3>Provide Borrower Information</h3>
        <p>Enter details about the borrower, including income sources, existing debts, and additional owners if applicable. Include information about lease expiry and mortgage rank if relevant.</p>

        <h2>Step 4: Completing the Loan Application</h2>
        <h3>Upload Required Documents</h3>
        <p>Upload necessary documents such as the contract of sale, proof of existence, discharge forms, and IDs. Ensure all documents are uploaded through the software for security reasons.</p>

        <h3>Final Review and Submission</h3>
        <p>Review all the information entered before submission. Click “Submit” to finalize and send your application to Titan Capital.</p>

        <h3>Immediate Response</h3>
        <p>After submission, the system will provide an immediate response:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Letter of Offer (LOO) Issued:</strong> If approved, you’ll receive an LOO.</li>
            <li><strong>Rejected:</strong> If not approved, you’ll be notified immediately.</li>
            <li><strong>Pending Credit Review:</strong> If further review is needed, the application will be sent to the Credit Team.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Final Steps</h2>
        <p>If the loan is approved, the final loan amount will be calculated, and relevant parties will be informed. Titan Capital aims to provide a smooth, transparent process that enables brokers to manage loan applications effectively.</p>
